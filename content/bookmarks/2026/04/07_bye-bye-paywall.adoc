The tool lets users access cached versions of webpages through third-party services, which can sometimes bypass paywalls. It emphasizes that it does not provide illegal services and that users are responsible for how they use these external tools.

* Enter the URL of the page you want to view
* Choose one of the available services
* Get redirected to a cached version of the page without the paywall
